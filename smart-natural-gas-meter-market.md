<p><ul><li>AMR</li><li>AMI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Natural Gas Meter market comprises two main types: Automated Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR systems enable utility companies to collect meter data remotely, enhancing efficiency and reducing manual readings. In contrast, AMI systems provide a more comprehensive approach, integrating real-time data monitoring, two-way communication, and advanced analytics. This allows utilities to optimize energy distribution, detect leaks, and improve customer engagement, leading to better resource management and enhanced service delivery in the natural gas sector.</p></p>
