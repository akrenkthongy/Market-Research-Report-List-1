<p><strong>The Automatic Motor Starters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Star Delta Starter</li><li>Direct-On-Line Starter</li><li>Rotor Resistance Starter</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic motor starters market comprises several types, including Star Delta Starters, Direct-On-Line Starters, and Rotor Resistance Starters. Star Delta Starters reduce starting current by initially connecting the motor in a star configuration before switching to delta for normal operation. Direct-On-Line Starters connect the motor directly to the power supply, providing full voltage and high starting torque. Rotor Resistance Starters involve adjusting the resistance in the rotor circuit to control starting current and torque. Other types include soft starters and variable frequency drives, catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
