<p><strong>The Jockey Box Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coil Cooler</li><li>Cold-plate Cooler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jockey box market types include coil coolers and cold-plate coolers, both designed for rapid beverage cooling. Coil coolers use a coiled stainless steel tubing through which cold water circulates, chilling the beverage as it flows. They are often compact and portable, ideal for outdoor events. Cold-plate coolers, on the other hand, feature a flat metal plate that is chilled prior, allowing beverages to cool as they pass over it. Both systems enhance serving efficiency and are popular in catering and brewery settings.</p></p>
