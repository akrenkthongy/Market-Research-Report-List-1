<p><p>The fracking fluid and chemicals market is primarily categorized into three types: water-based, oil-gel based, and foam-based. Water-based fluids are the most commonly used, comprising mainly water and additives to enhance efficiency. Oil-gel based fluids utilize oil as their primary carrier, providing better viscosity and temperature stability for challenging formations. Foam-based fluids consist of gas and liquid, offering reduced water usage and improved mobility in low-permeability environments. Each type serves specific geological conditions and operational requirements in hydraulic fracturing.</p></p>
