<p><p>The composite resins market is primarily divided into two types: thermoplastics and thermosetting resins. Thermoplastic resins can be repeatedly melted and reformed, making them versatile for various applications, such as automotive and aerospace components. In contrast, thermosetting resins undergo a curing process that permanently hardens them upon application of heat, enhancing their rigidity and chemical resistance, ideal for applications like electronics and construction. Each type caters to specific needs, driving innovation and growth in their respective sectors.</p></p>
