<p><p>The tool spindles market is categorized into several types based on their drive mechanisms. Gear-driven spindles utilize gears to transfer power, offering high torque and stability for heavy machining. Belt-driven spindles use belts to connect the motor and spindle, providing flexibility in speed adjustments. Direct-driven spindles connect the motor directly to the spindle, resulting in precise control and minimal maintenance. Additionally, the "Others" category includes specialized spindle designs that may incorporate unique technologies or serve niche applications, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
