<p><p>The underwater exploration robotic machine market is divided into two primary types: light work and heavy work. In contrast, heavy work robotic machines are built for more demanding operations, including construction, salvage, and deep-sea exploration. These machines are larger, equipped with advanced tools, and capable of withstanding significant underwater pressures and harsh conditions.</p></p>
