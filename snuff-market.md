<p><p>The snuff market is primarily divided into moist and dry snuff. Moist snuff, often referred to as dip, is finely ground tobacco that is typically packed in small pouches or containers, designed for oral use without combustion. It offers a wetter texture and a stronger flavor profile. In contrast, dry snuff consists of coarse tobacco that is inhaled through the nose. It is generally less moist and is available in various scents and flavors, appealing to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
