<p><p>Axle shaft bearings are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I bearings are typically designed for light to moderate load applications, offering basic support and rotation for various vehicle axles. They prioritize cost-effectiveness and ease of maintenance. On the other hand, Type II bearings are engineered for heavy-duty applications, providing enhanced durability and performance under higher stress and load conditions. This type is often used in commercial and industrial vehicles, ensuring reliability and longevity in challenging environments.</p></p>
