<p><p>Laboratory Information Systems (LIS) can be categorized into three main market types: on-premise, web-based, and cloud-based. On-premise LIS are installed locally on a facility's servers, offering direct control but requiring significant IT resources. Web-based LIS are accessible via browsers, enabling remote access and easier updates while reducing local infrastructure needs. Cloud-based LIS leverage internet storage to provide scalability and flexibility, allowing labs to access data anytime and anywhere, often with lower upfront costs and maintenance responsibilities.</p></p>
