<p><p>The fluoropolymer membrane market encompasses various types of membranes, each with distinct properties and applications. PFSA (Perfluorosulfonic Acid) membranes are known for their proton conductivity in fuel cells. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) offers excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) membranes provide good non-stick properties. ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) membranes are lightweight and durable. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) membranes are used in filtration. PCTFE (Polychlorotrifluoroethylene) membranes are highly resistant to harsh chemicals, while "Others" include specialized formulations and blends for niche uses.</p></p>
